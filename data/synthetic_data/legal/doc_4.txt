Overview of Patent Application No. 2023/346: The invention pertains to an advanced energy-efficient semiconductor cooling system designed to improve the thermal management of high-performance computing devices. The system employs a novel microfluidic approach, ensuring minimal energy dissipation while maintaining optimal operating temperatures under peak loads. Extensive simulations have demonstrated a reduction in energy consumption by approximately 30% compared to current industry standards. The Patent Cooperation Treaty (PCT) application includes 15 claims outlining the system's structure, method of operation, and potential integrations into existing manufacturing processes. Examination of the application is underway, with a projected completion timeline of Q1 2024.
