School Board Meeting Minutes: January 15, 2023

The York County School Board held its regular meeting on January 15, 2023, at the Central Administration Office. The meeting commenced at 7 PM with all members present.

The primary agenda item discussed was the proposal for new curriculum standards in mathematics. Dr. Susan Patel presented a comprehensive review highlighting the need for updated materials and integration of technology in classrooms.

Public comments were welcomed, with several community members expressing support for the proposed changes. The board unanimously voted to approve the curriculum enhancements, which will be implemented in the upcoming academic year.

Other business included budget allocations for facility improvements and upcoming extracurricular activities. The meeting adjourned at 9 PM.
