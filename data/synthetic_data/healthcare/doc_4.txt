Public Health Announcement: Influenza Vaccination Clinic Openings. The Health Department is pleased to announce the opening of influenza vaccination clinics across the county starting November 1st. Clinics will be available at community centers, schools, and designated hospitals. Vaccinations are free of charge for all residents. This year, the focus is on increasing vaccination rates among children and seniors. Special pop-up clinics will be set up in underserved areas. Residents are encouraged to schedule their vaccinations online or walk into any of the clinics. Preventing flu outbreaks is a community effort, and we thank everyone for participating.
