School Policy Document: Anti-Bullying Policy

Our school is committed to providing a safe and respectful learning environment for all students. Bullying is strictly prohibited and will not be tolerated. This policy covers bullying that occurs on school premises, at school-sponsored activities, and online.

Definitions of bullying include, but are not limited to, physical harm, verbal harassment, and cyberbullying. Any student who believes they have been subject to or have witnessed bullying must report the incident to the school principal or a trusted faculty member immediately.

Consequences for violations include disciplinary measures that may range from loss of privileges to suspension. The school will provide educational programs and workshops aimed at fostering a culture of respect and inclusion.
