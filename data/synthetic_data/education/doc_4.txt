Student Support Services Description

Our university is dedicated to supporting the academic and personal success of our students. The Student Support Services (SSS) department offers a range of resources including academic advising, tutoring, career counseling, and mental health services.

Advising sessions are available to help students plan their academic trajectory and achieve their career goals. Tutoring services cover a wide range of subjects from mathematics to humanities, offering one-on-one sessions with experienced tutors.

Our mental health services include confidential counseling and workshops on managing stress and anxiety. Students are encouraged to reach out to the Student Support Services at any time to ensure their well-being and success.
