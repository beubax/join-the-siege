An educational curriculum proposal submitted to the Department of Education outlines a comprehensive approach to integrating computer science into high school classes. With technology proficiency becoming essential for future careers, the proposed curriculum includes modules on programming, cybersecurity, and data analytics. Educators will receive specialized training to deliver these courses effectively, ensuring students gain hands-on experience with current tools and technologies. The curriculum aims to bridge the digital skills gap and foster critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and innovation. The proposal highlights partnerships with industry leaders to provide mentorship opportunities and access to cutting-edge resources for students.
