Subject: Regulatory Compliance Briefing - March 2023

Attention: Compliance Officers

This month's regulatory briefing focuses on recent amendments to the Anti-Money Laundering Act. The key changes include stringent KYC norms and enhanced reporting requirements for high-value transactions. All affected departments must review and update their compliance protocols accordingly. Training sessions will be scheduled to familiarize staff with these changes. Non-compliance could attract significant legal penalties and damage the company's reputation.
