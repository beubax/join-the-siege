Hospital Internal Memo: Attention: All Staff. Subject: Mandatory Training on Infection Control. As part of our ongoing efforts to maintain the highest standards of patient safety, all medical and non-medical staff are required to complete the updated infection control training by December 15th. The training module is available online and includes a quiz which must be completed to ensure compliance. Our infection control measures are crucial in preventing hospital-acquired infections and safeguarding both patients and staff. Please contact the infection control department for any questions or assistance.
