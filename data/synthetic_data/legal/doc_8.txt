Course Syllabus Excerpt: Artificial Intelligence in Modern Business Applications. Course Overview: This course explores the deployment of artificial intelligence (AI) techniques in contemporary business environments. Topics covered include machine learning algorithms, natural language processing, AI ethics, and the impact of AI on employment patterns. Course Objectives: By the end of this course, students should be able to develop basic AI models, assess the societal implications of AI deployment, and propose solutions to potential ethical dilemmas. Assessment will be based on project work, group discussions, and a final examination scheduled for December 12, 2023.
