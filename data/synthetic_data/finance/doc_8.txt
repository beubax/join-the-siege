Minutes of the Investment Committee Meeting held on June 20, 2023

The meeting commenced with a review of the current portfolio performance, noting an overall increase in equity returns. The committee discussed reallocating some assets to alternative investments, including real estate funds, for better risk management. Considerations also included potential divestment from underperforming stocks in the commodities sector. It was agreed to explore new opportunities in the biotech industry, given its robust growth potential and innovation trends.
