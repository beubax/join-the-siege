EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance Training Manual: This manual has been developed to provide comprehensive guidelines for ensuring compliance with GDPR. Key aspects include the classification of personal data, requirements for obtaining valid consent, processes for securing data subject rights, and protocols for data breach notifications. All staff handling personal data are required to review this manual thoroughly and complete the enclosed assessment. Commitment to GDPR compliance is crucial in maintaining the trust of clients and upholding the reputation of the organization in data privacy matters.
