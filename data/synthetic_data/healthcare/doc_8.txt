Educational Health Course Syllabus Excerpt: Course Title: Introduction to Public Health. Course Description: This course provides an overview of public health concepts, focusing on the principles and practices that promote and protect the health of communities. Curriculum includes the study of epidemiology, biostatistics, health policy, environmental health, and social determinants of health. Learning Objectives: Students will understand the fundamental principles of public health, recognize the role of public health in disease prevention and health promotion, and analyze public health data to assess community health needs. Assessment: Evaluation will be based on quizzes, a midterm paper, and participation in group projects. Recommended Reading: 'Introduction to Public Health,' 5th Edition by Mary J. Schneider.
