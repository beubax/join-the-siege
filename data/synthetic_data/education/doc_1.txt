Course Syllabus: Introduction to Psychology

Welcome to Introduction to Psychology! This course provides a comprehensive overview of fundamental psychological concepts. We will cover topics including history of psychology, development, cognitive processes, perception, and social behavior. Each week, students are expected to engage with both the lecture material and assigned readings.

The course will be divided into 15 weeks, with mid-term and final exams. Participation in discussions and group projects is mandatory. Office hours are available weekly for additional support.

Assessment will include quizzes, assignments, and active participation in class activities. The required textbook is "Psychology: Themes and Variations" by Wayne Weiten. Students must complete all assignments by the deadlines outlined in the syllabus to pass the course.
