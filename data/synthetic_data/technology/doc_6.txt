This patent application outlines an innovative method for real-time monitoring of environmental pollutants using IoT-enabled sensors. By deploying a network of low-cost, wireless sensors, the invention facilitates continuous air quality measurement and data transmission to centralized databases. This approach allows for dynamic mapping of pollution levels, enabling authorities to implement timely interventions. The document details the sensor architecture, communication protocols, and data analytics framework, designed to optimize resource utilization and enhance scalability. The application underscores the potential impact on public health, urban planning, and regulatory compliance, paving the way for smarter cities and cleaner environments.
