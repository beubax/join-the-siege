Educational Conference Announcement: Innovations in Teaching

Join us for the annual Innovations in Teaching Conference, which will be held on March 25-27, 2023, at the Downtown Conference Center. This event gathers educators and administrators from around the world to explore new teaching methodologies and technologies.

Featured keynote speakers include Dr. Jane Mills, an expert in educational technology, and Professor Liang Sun, specializing in pedagogical innovations. Workshops cover a range of topics such as digital classrooms, inclusive education strategies, and student assessment.

Attendees will have opportunities to network, share experiences, and gain insights into cutting-edge tools that can be integrated into their teaching practice. Registration is open until March 1, 2023.
